Bio

Ivan Alexander Zapata is a student at Howard Community College studying to receive a degree in audio-video media production (AVMP). Ivan has been interested in media ever since he could remember, whether it was related to animations, shows, films, etc. He would record skits and other types of entertainment videos and would upload them onto YouTube when he was younger. When he was in high school he continued this interest by joining a TV production class as well as entering multiple film contests, one of which he would win third place alongside his team. While in college, he would also be introduced to the world of sound design and editing, where he now works with his peers and others to create music and film sound effects. Ivan's goal in life is to use his talents to help others grow and make an impact on the world.
